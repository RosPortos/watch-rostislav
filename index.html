The Impact of Rolex The Impact of Rolex

Rolex, over the years, has redefined the horological industry with its groundbreaking innovations and unparalleled craftsmanship.

From introducing the first waterproof wristwatch to pioneering the automatic date changing mechanism, Rolex's contributions to watchmaking are unparalleled.

The Legacy Behind the Timepiece

Founded in 1905, Rolex's journey from a small watch company to a global luxury icon is filled with milestones and achievements.
  • - 1905: Rolex founded.
  • - 1945: Launch of the Datejust, the first automatic date wristwatch.
  • - 1926: Introduction of the first waterproof wristwatch
